# Introduction

Welcome to ChatDB!

Welcome to the documentation for **ChatDB** - your go-to AI assistant designed to simplify and streamline database interactions.

## What is ChatDB?

ChatDB is a platform to make your data accessible for both non technical and technical users. No more SQL, just ask your question to the database and receive results directly from the database.
